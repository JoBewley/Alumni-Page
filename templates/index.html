Stop By!

Our Alumni Association office is located in the D Building, Room D34067. Feel free to stop by during our office hours from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday. Take the main elevator or stair case in the Hall of Governors to the third floor, turn left, then right at the end of the hall. You will see a decorated table outside of our office. It is the only one in the hallway so you cannot miss us!
